Who We Are

A Decade of
Star Stories

Founded in 2013, Astro Club BIT Durg grew from a handful of students who refused to stop looking up. Today we are Central India's most active student astronomy community.

We replace superstition with science, fear with wonder, and ignorance with curiosity. The universe belongs to everyone who dares to ask.

What We Do

Our Activities

From telescope handling to ISRO guest lectures — every activity ignites curiosity.

Stargazing Sessions

Regular nighttime events exploring constellations, planets, and deep-sky objects under Chhattisgarh's open sky.

Telescope Handling

Practical workshops on assembling, aligning, and using telescopes — from refractors to our club Dobsonian reflector.

Workshops & Labs

Astrophotography, spectroscopy, radio astronomy, and computational tools like Stellarium and FITS viewers.

Guest Lectures

Talks by ISRO scientists, professors, and astronomers bringing the frontiers of space science to campus.

School Outreach

We carry telescopes to local schools, inspiring the next generation and breaking superstitions through direct observation.

Research Projects

Student-led observational research published in journals and presented at national-level science fests.
